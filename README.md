### 📖 About the Project

**[Sword of Sikhs](https://sword-of-sikhs.vercel.app/)** is an educational and historical web platform dedicated to exploring the epic struggles in Sikh-Mughal power dynamics. The project details the birth of Sikhism, the ensuing Mughal influence, and the resilient Sikh response, preserving the rich history of the Khalsa. 

The site features deep dives into pivotal historical conflicts, including:
*   **The Battle of Rohilla (1621)**
*   **The Battle of Chamkaur (1704)**
*   **The Battle of Chappar Chiri (1710)**

### ✨ Key Features

*   **Immersive Storytelling:** Detailed, well-researched accounts of major historical battles and the heroism of key figures like Guru Hargobind, Guru Gobind Singh, and Banda Singh Bahadur.
*   **Rich Historical Context:** Explores the theological and social origins of Sikhism to give users a complete picture of the era.
*   **Responsive UI:** A beautifully styled web interface ensuring a seamless reading experience across devices.
